About us

Get to Know Meshari Group

Meshari Group is a leading food product company that has been operating for over 20 years. We are committed to producing high-quality, tasty, and nutritious products that are enjoyed by people all around the world.
At Meshari Group, we believe in using only the freshest ingredients and the most advanced production techniques to create products that meet the highest standards of quality and taste. We are constantly innovating and introducing new products to meet the changing needs of our customers.
Our extensive range of products includes a variety of staple foods, such as rice, pasta, and flour, as well as a range of specialty products like artisanal breads, premium olive oils, and gourmet chocolates.
We are proud to be a socially responsible company that values sustainability, ethical sourcing, and the well-being of our employees and customers. We work closely with local communities and suppliers to ensure that our products are produced in an environmentally friendly manner.

Mission

Our mission at Meshari Group is to provide high-quality, nourishing food products that meet the needs and preferences of our customers. We strive to use sustainable, natural ingredients and practices in all of our products, while maintaining the highest standards of food safety and production.

Vision

Our vision is to be the leading food product company in the region, known for our commitment to health, sustainability, and innovation. We aim to expand our reach and impact by introducing new and innovative products, as well as expanding our distribution channels and partnerships. We also envision a future where our products are accessible to all, regardless of income or location.
